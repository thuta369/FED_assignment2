Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

You can create an account and start listing or buying items immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How can I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?" to receive a reset link.

Is there a fee to use this platform?

Creating an account is free, but we charge a small fee for certain transactions.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@example.com or through our live chat feature.

Can I change my username?

Usernames cannot be changed after registration for security reasons.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the seller, but typically range from 3-7 business days.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies depend on the seller. Please check their return policy before making a purchase.

How do I report a fraudulent listing?

You can report suspicious listings by clicking the "Report" button on the item page.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support.
